Why 6.3? The answer is deeply rooted in our commitment to precision and excellence. 6.3 represents the pH level of human sweat—a testament to the natural processes that occur when our bodies are in motion. This number symbolizes balance, harmony, and the essence of human performance. Just as the pH level of sweat is vital to maintaining our body's equilibrium, Six Point Three is dedicated to maintaining the perfect balance between comfort and activeness in our apparel.
